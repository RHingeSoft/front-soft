    General Questions

    What is Lazo ?

    Robin is a virtual assistant designed and trained to follow an instruction in a prompt and provide a detailed response. Robin offers information in a clear and understandable way to individuals, teams or companies from different sectors. It is a chat platform that allows you to provide detailed information on specialized topics, complex processes, content, data and specific information.

    Why do we use it ?

    Through this exchange of information, Robin will become an expert assistant in the subject area of your choice, providing advice and specialized knowledge in an accessible way at any time.

    How is it possible ?

    Robin uses NLP techniques to understand and process human language. This allows you to analyze the instructions and questions provided by users and generate coherent and understandable responses.
